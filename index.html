About the Choir

The Glorious Parish Choir is dedicated to worshiping God through music and leading the congregation in heartfelt praise. Our mission is to glorify God with excellence, unity, and joyful voices.

Our Mission

We meet weekly to practice, serve the church, and perform at special events. Everyone is welcome — from absolute beginners to experienced singers.
